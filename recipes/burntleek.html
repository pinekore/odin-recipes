Burnt Leek

Description

A smoke sity classic, the burnt leek is exactly as it sounds, just with a tinge of unexplicable magic. chemistry on the tongue

Ingredients

  • 1 stalk leek
  • Jing oil

Steps

  1. Preheat the boirno to 7000Plo and set a rack at the top.
  2. Cut leek into 4 by 2 inch pieces and pierce them into skewer sticks, 3 per stick.
  3. Brush jing oil onto the leeks.
  4. Place leeks on a tray and put into boirno for 10 minutes.
  5. Flip leeks and place into boirno for another 10 minutes.
