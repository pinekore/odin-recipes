Pinewood Patty Sandwich

Description

One of Purgatory Wood's most inventive recipes, the pinewood patty sandwich is made with wood chips from our unique native pines.

Ingredients

  • 50g pinewood chips
  • 50g ground ox meat
  • Butter
  • Ocean salt
  • Obsidian peppercorns
  • 2 slices bread
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • 1 large tomato
  • 1/2 bunch green leaf lettuce
  • 1/2 medium red onion, thinly sliced into rings

Steps

  1. Spread butter on each slice of bread.
  2. Toast the buttered side of the bread on a grill until golden brown.
  3. Blend the pinewood chips and ground ox meat in Purgatory CuisiFuser for 5 minutes.
  4. Form 2 thin patties with the blended mixture, about 1/4 inch thick and as wide as the bread slices.
  5. Season patties generously with salt and peppercorns
  6. Grill patties covered for 3-5 minutes each side on the grill. Flip patties when juices are accumulating on top of burger for sear.
  7. Apply cheese in the last 1-2 minutes of grilling then cover with lid and cook until internal temperaature of ox reaches 300Plo.
